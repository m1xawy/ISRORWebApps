Buy Item Guide

On Silkroad Online, items are categorizedinto tradable and untradeable items.

Tradable Items

  • Tradable items are sold at the Premium Mall.
  • They can only purchased with the Premium Silk().
  • They cannot be purchased with Silk.

Untradeable Items

  • Untradeable items are sold at the Silk Mall
  • They can be purchased either with Silk() or with Premium Silk().
  • Upon purchase, your Silk will be deducted automatically in the order of Silk > Premium Silk.

Silk Information

The digital currency on Silkroad Online is divided into Silk and Premium Silk.
Depending on which Silk you use, you will be limited in your item selections and gifting options.

What is Silk()?

  • Silk is the digital currency of Silkroad Online used to purchase premium items.
  • With Silk, you can purchase untradeable items.
  • Silk Payment Methods : PayPal, Click & Buy (Euro), Click & Buy (USD), Direct Payment, Moneybookers, Credit Card

What is Premium Silk()?

  • Premium Silk is the digital currency of Silkroad Online used to purchase premium items..
  • There is no limit to item purchases.
    (You can purchase both tradable and untradeable items.)
  • Premium Silk can also be gifted to other players.
    (You can purchase items with Premium Silk that you receive as a gift.)
  • Premium Silk Payment Methods: PaybyCash, Incomm, CashU, Paysafecard, Wallie-card, E-Pin (All)

Receiving Purchased Items

  • 1) Start the game and enter the server selected upon purchasing the item.
  • 2) Enter the game with the character who will receive the item.
  • 3) View the item list in the notification window to the bottom left corner of the screen, and move the item(s) into your inventory.
